Where Is Shimla?

First things first -- where is Shimla on the world map?

Shimla is a beautiful hill station located in the northern state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Nestled in the western Himalayas, Shimla is known for its stunning landscapes and colonial architecture.

Shimla is situated approximately 342 kilometers (213 miles) north of the national capital, New Delhi. It was once the summer capital of British India and remains a popular tourist destination for its pleasant climate and scenic views.
