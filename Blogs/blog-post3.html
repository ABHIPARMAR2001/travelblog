Chikkamagaluru

Chikkamagaluru, a charming hill station in Karnataka, is famous for its coffee plantations, lush greenery, and serene landscapes.
The town’s name translates to "Younger Daughter's Town," with a historical backstory linked to a local chieftain’s tradition of gifting land to his daughters.

Must-Visit Places in Coorg

1. Mullayanagiri Peak

Description :The highest peak in Karnataka at 1,930 meters, offering stunning views of the Western Ghats.
Wear comfortable shoes and carry water ,the trail involves a steep climb.

2.Kudremukh National Park

Description: A beautiful wildlife sanctuary with a diverse ecosystem, featuring dense forests, rivers, and waterfalls.
Entry is regulated it’s best to check timings and permits.

Frequently asked question

1.Is it safe to trek in Chikkamagaluru?
Ans:Yes, trekking is generally safe. Popular treks like Mullayanagiri and Baba Budangiri have marked trails, but it’s recommended to go with a guide, especially if trekking in lesser-known areas.

2. What are the must-visit places in Chikkamagaluru?
Ans: Mullayanagiri Peak, Baba Budangiri, Kudremukh National Park, Hebbe Falls, Jhari Waterfalls, and Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary are among the top attractions.:
