Coorg (Kodagu)

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a scenic hill station in Karnataka, India, celebrated for its lush coffee plantations, misty landscapes, and vibrant culture. Known as the "Scotland of India,".

Must-Visit Places in Coorg

1. Abbey Falls

Description :A beautiful waterfall surrounded by coffee plantations. The walk to the falls is scenic, with the sound of water enhancing the experience.

2. Dubare Elephant Camp

Description: An elephant training camp where you can watch and interact with elephants in a natural setting. Activities include bathing and feeding elephants.

Frequently asked question

1. How can I reach Coorg?
Ans: Coorg does not have a railway station or airport. The nearest airport is in Mysore (120 km) or Mangalore (135 km). You can also drive from Bangalore (approx. 5 hours), as many tourists prefer traveling by car.

2. What is Coorg famous for?
Ans :Coorg is famous for its coffee plantations, scenic landscapes, spices, honey, and unique Kodava culture. The region also produces high-quality pepper and cardamom.
