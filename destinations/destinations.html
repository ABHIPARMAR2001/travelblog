Jaipur

Jaipur, often called the "Pink City" due to its stunning terracotta-colored buildings, is the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, India. Known for its royal heritage, Jaipur is a treasure trove of majestic forts, ornate palaces, and colorful bazaars.

Vishakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, or Vizag, is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning beaches, lush green hills, and cultural heritage. Key attractions include the scenic RK Beach, the ancient Borra Caves, and the serene Araku Valley nearby. Ideal for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike, Vizag offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Shimla

Shimla, the charming hill station and capital of Himachal Pradesh, is renowned for its cool climate, colonial architecture, and scenic mountain views. Visitors can explore the famous Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and the lush greenery of Kufri. Shimla offers a peaceful escape with a touch of adventure and history.
